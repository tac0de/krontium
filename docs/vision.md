# Krontium Vision

## Current Web Positioning

Krontium is a no-text mineral generative art site. The word should feel like a discovered material, an ore body under pressure, or a black crystalline object emitting heat from inside.

## Source Motifs

Use:

- black mineral core
- crystalline shards
- accretion-like dust
- ore veins
- oxide green-blue light
- ember inclusions
- pressure rings
- slow rotational mass

## Tone

Krontium should feel precise, cold, dense, mineral, and non-template. The first read should be material rather than narrative.

Use:

- Three.js or shader-driven browser composition
- CSS for framing, darkness, and pressure
- textless composition
- controlled motion
- crystalline geometry
- black, graphite, bone-gold, oxidized green-blue, and ember accents

Avoid:

- conventional landing pages
- explanatory presentation
- genre framing
- generic cards
- cute objects or mascot forms
- hand-wavy abstract decoration
- visible explanation text
- UI chrome that weakens the artwork

## Website Experience

The first screen is the artwork. The user should not feel they arrived at a blog, SaaS landing page, or template. Compatibility routes may exist, but the site itself is textless by default.

Primary actions:

- inspect motion and pressure
- feel the mineral wordmark without visible words
- treat the browser surface as the artwork
